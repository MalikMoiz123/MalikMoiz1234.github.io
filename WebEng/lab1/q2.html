Pancakes

Description: Delicious pancakes served with maple syrup.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Whisk milk, melted butter, and egg.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Cook pancakes on a hot griddle.
  5. Serve with maple syrup.
